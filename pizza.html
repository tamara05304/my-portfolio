No-Fail Pizza

Perfecting dough fermentation for optimal texture and flavor

Ingredients

For the base:

  • 250g wholemeal flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast (Saccharomyces cerevisiae)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp rapeseed oil

For the topping:

  • 3 peppers (mixed colors)
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tbsp rapeseed oil
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds (Foeniculum vulgare)
  • 2 tbsp barbecue sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 225g skinless chicken breast, diced
  • 175g baby plum tomatoes, quartered
  • 50g Applewood smoked cheese, grated

Method

Step 1: Make the Dough

Heat oven to 220°C/200°C fan/gas 7. Mix flour, yeast, salt, oil and 200ml warm water to a very soft dough. Knead for 5 mins in mixer or 10 mins by hand (it's sticky but don't add too much extra flour). Cover and let rest while you prep toppings.

Step 2: Roast Veggies

Toss sliced peppers and onions with oil and fennel seeds, roast for 15 mins. Mix barbecue sauce and tomato purée with 5 tbsp water.

Step 3: Assemble

Press dough into oiled 25x35cm tin (don't knead first!). Spread with ⅔ sauce, coat chicken with remaining. Top with roasted veggies, tomatoes, chicken, and cheese.

Step 4: Bake & Serve

Bake for 15 mins until golden. Serve with salad or coleslaw.

The Science Behind This Recipe

  • The fermentation time allows yeast to produce CO₂ which creates air pockets in the dough
  • Wholemeal flour contains more nutrients for yeast compared to white flour
  • Resting the dough allows gluten strands to relax, making it easier to shape
  • The high oven temperature creates Maillard reactions for optimal browning
  • Fennel seeds contain antimicrobial compounds that may help with digestion
